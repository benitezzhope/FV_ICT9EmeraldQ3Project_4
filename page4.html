Passenger Tips & Reminders



  • Gear Up
    • Use a certified helmet (full-face, modular, or open-face with visor).

  • Positioning
    • Mount from the left side, keep your feet on the footpegs, and avoid wiggling or shifting weight unexpectedly.

  • Active Passenger
    • Follow the rider's movements during turns and leans to maintain balance.

  • Communication
    • Tap the rider's shoulder to communicate, ask to slow down if necessary, or discuss the route before starting.

  • Health Protocols
    • Sanitize hands and wear masks as required by safety guidelines.

  • Be Prepared
    • Inform friends or family of your ride details.

  • Weight Check
    • Ensure the rider is comfortable with your weight to handle the bike safely.

  • Weather Prep
    • Be aware of weather conditions and prepare appropriate gear.
